Supported Applications

MindControl works with every app on your device. You decide what stays and what goes.

How it works

MindControl uses Android's Accessibility Services to monitor active applications in real-time. This allows us to:

  • Identify distracting social media apps
  • Block instant messaging during work hours
  • Prevent endless scrolling on entertainment platforms
  • Ensure only productivity tools are accessible

By default, MindControl allows you to select 5 essential apps (including the Phone app for emergencies) that will remain accessible during your focus session.

Social Media

Block Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and more with zero lag.

Entertainment

Prevent YouTube or Netflix binges when you should be working.

Messaging

Silence WhatsApp, Telegram, or Discord to avoid constant pings.
