Keep My Phone Out - Last updated: January 29, 2026

Summary: Keep My Phone Out does NOT collect, store, or share any personal data. All data stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

Keep My Phone Out is designed with your privacy in mind. We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers

2. Data Storage

All app data, including your focus session history and preferences, is stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to any external servers.

3. Permissions We Use

The app requires certain permissions to function:

  • Accessibility Service: Used to detect which app is in the foreground during focus sessions. This is required to help you stay focused.
  • Notification Permission: Used to show timer countdown notifications during focus sessions.
  • Usage Access: Required to identify installed apps for the app blocking feature.

None of this data leaves your device.

4. Advertising

The app may display ads through Google AdMob. Google's advertising partners may collect device information for ad personalization. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more details.

5. Children's Privacy

Keep My Phone Out is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
